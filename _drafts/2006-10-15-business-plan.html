I mean I can handle the computers and the systems and the electronic litter all over the place, but creating a Business Plan - WOW! That's Hard!

I know that people have speciallities and that some people look at a computer and panic. Now I know how they feel. I have all these ideas and thoughts in my head, but when I sit down to put them in to the BP (see, I'm even applying TLAs to it!) they just scatter. Kind of like when you go into a music store you can't remember any artist that you like.

All I know is that I have a new found respect for business people that can sit down and shovel out this drivel all day long. I think my personal hell would be to sit in a small cubical, with a coat & tie on, and prepare documentation all day long. My current job is not too far from that, but at least I can wear jeans - or rather I can wear jeans as long as they don't say anything to me about it.

sigh...
