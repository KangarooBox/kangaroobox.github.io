Philosophy

KangarooBox is unique in that our solutions include everything you need, all in one box. We believe in the K.I.S.S. (Keep It Stupid Simple) design philosophy and strive to make your whole experience easy.

Software

All of our products are web based systems that are operated from a simple browser. If your reading this you already have all the equipment you need to get started.

Hardware

Our server appliance is a small box that can be placed almost anywhere; all it needs is a nearby network connection and an electrical outlet. It can be placed on a desk, or a bookshelf, or inside a locked drawer (for extra security). And if you ever need to move it or take it with you, just unplug it and go.

Products

Dingo Discontinued

The Dingo system is an RMS (record management system) for law enforcement agencies. It is designed to be very simple to use and includes unique features.

Kookaburra Discontinued

Kookaburra is a school grading system designed by teachers, for teachers. It gives students and parents access to grades and report cards throughout the year and allows teachers an easy way to keep everything up-to-date.
