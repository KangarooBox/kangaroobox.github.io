Our Team

Richard Hurt - Founder (GitHub, LinkedIn, Mastodon)

Richard has been programming computers since he was 7 years old. Spending all day at the local Radio Shack playing with the new Color Computer models and learning all about loops and if..then..else statements was a day spent well. After graduating from JB Speed School of Engineering with a degree in Information Science and Data Processing he started writing computer programs in the business world.

Eventually he couldn't resist the siren call of entrepreneurship and he left the corporate world behind and started KangarooBox. This new company would make Information Technology easy to understand and use for the less technically inclined.
